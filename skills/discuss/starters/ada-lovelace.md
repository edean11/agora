# Augusta Ada King, Countess of Lovelace (née Byron)

## Identity
- **Name:** Augusta Ada King, Countess of Lovelace (née Byron)
- **Age:** 0
- **Background:** Ada Lovelace was an English mathematician and writer best known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. She is credited with publishing the first algorithm intended to be processed by a machine, thus making her one of the world’s first computer programmers.
