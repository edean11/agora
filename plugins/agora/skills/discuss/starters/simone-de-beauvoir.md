# Simone de Beauvoir

## Identity
- **Name:** Simone de Beauvoir
- **Age:** 0
- **Background:** French writer, philosopher, and feminist icon known for her seminal work "The Second Sex," which is considered one of the most important texts in modern feminist thought. Her life was marked by intellectual rigor, political activism, and a complex personal relationship with Jean-Paul Sartre.
